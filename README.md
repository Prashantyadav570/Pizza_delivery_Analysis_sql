# 🍕 Pizza Delivery SQL Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a Pizza Delivery dataset using **MySQL** to solve real-world business problems and generate actionable insights. The analysis includes data exploration, KPI calculation, restaurant performance evaluation, delivery efficiency analysis, customer ordering patterns, traffic impact, and delay analysis.

A total of **50+ SQL business questions** were solved using SQL concepts ranging from basic queries to advanced analytical functions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze delivery performance across restaurants.
- Measure delivery efficiency and delays.
- Identify customer ordering trends.
- Evaluate the impact of traffic and peak hours.
- Calculate business KPIs.
- Generate business recommendations based on data.

## 💡 Business Insights

- High traffic significantly increases delivery time.
- Peak hours experience the highest number of delivery delays.
- Some restaurants consistently perform better than others.
- Card payments are the most preferred payment method.
- Long-distance deliveries have a higher probability of delays.
- Weekend deliveries take longer on average compared to weekdays.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Increase delivery staff during peak hours.
- Optimize delivery routes in high-traffic areas.
- Improve restaurant preparation time for slow-performing restaurants.
- Introduce promotional offers during non-peak hours.
- Monitor restaurants with consistently high delivery delays.
